Autobiography


Meteor V. Ordoña is 20-years Old, a student of University of Northern Philippines, Vigan City Ilocos Sur taking the curriculum of Bachelor of Science of Computer Science. He is from Candon City, Ilocos Sur. While studying, he take advantage of the online class due to the pandemic, to be employed as a Marketing Manager on a start-up software development company, Unirises located at Quezon City.

He is a young entrepreneur who starts his own business with the help of his two cousin. They build a start-up clothing brand in their hometown, Candon City Ilocos Sur. WTG? Where's the Gold? is a brand that they founded, he is the designer, the production officer and also the digital marketer.

As a programmer, he finished ICT on his Senior High Program he created on a E-Learning System with his classmate for his final project. He also accept Freelance work like Website Design for a Simple Landing Page, He does this while studying on his Senior High Curriculum.

He is a Local rap artist that released almost 20 songs on YouTube. He mix, master and produce his own music without a help of engineer. He did this all while studying, because he believed that knowledge is free and available to everyone, that is the thing the can not be wasted.
